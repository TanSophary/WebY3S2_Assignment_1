User Email Setup Guidelines

Mozilla Thunderbird Email Setup

  1. Open Mozilla Thunderbird.
  2. Go to Account Settings > Add Mail Account.
  3. Enter your name, email (demo@your-name.com), and password.
  4. Click Continue and wait for auto-detection.
  5. If manual config is needed:
    • Incoming: IMAP, mail.your-name.com, Port: 993, SSL/TLS
    • Outgoing: SMTP, mail.your-name.com, Port: 465, SSL/TLS
  6. Click Done to finish setup.

Microsoft Outlook Email Setup

  1. Open Microsoft Outlook.
  2. Go to File > Add Account.
  3. Enter your email (demo@your-name.com).
  4. Click Connect and enter your password.
  5. If manual setup is required:
    • Incoming: mail.your-name.com, Port: 993, SSL/TLS
    • Outgoing: mail.your-name.com, Port: 465, SSL/TLS
  6. Click Next and finish setup.

Google Mail “Send As” Setup

  1. Log in to Gmail.
  2. Click the gear icon ⚙️ > See all settings.
  3. Go to Accounts and Import tab.
  4. Click Add another email address.
  5. Enter:
    • Name: Your Name
    • Email: demo@your-name.com
  6. Select Send through SMTP server:
    • SMTP: mail.your-name.com
    • Port: 465
    • Username: demo@your-name.com
    • Password: your password
    • Security: SSL
  7. Click Add Account and verify via email.
