Microsoft Outlook Email Setup

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Open Microsoft Outlook.
  2. Go to File > Add Account.
  3. Enter your email (demo@your-name.com).
  4. Click Connect and enter your password.
  5. If manual setup is required:
    • Incoming: mail.your-name.com, Port: 993, SSL/TLS
    • Outgoing: mail.your-name.com, Port: 465, SSL/TLS
  6. Click Next and finish setup.

Note: This is a demo guide (ទ.៥ គឺជាគំរូ).
