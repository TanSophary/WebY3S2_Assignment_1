Mozilla Thunderbird Email Setup

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Open Mozilla Thunderbird.
  2. Go to Account Settings > Add Mail Account.
  3. Enter your name, email (demo@your-name.com), and password.
  4. Click Continue and wait for auto-detection.
  5. If manual config is needed:
    • Incoming: IMAP, mail.your-name.com, Port: 993, SSL/TLS
    • Outgoing: SMTP, mail.your-name.com, Port: 465, SSL/TLS
  6. Click Done to finish setup.

Note: This is a demo guide (ទ.៥ គឺជាគំរូ).
