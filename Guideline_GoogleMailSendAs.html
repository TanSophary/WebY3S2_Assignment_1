Google Mail “Send As” Setup

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Log in to Gmail.
  2. Click the gear icon ⚙️ > See all settings.
  3. Go to Accounts and Import tab.
  4. Click Add another email address.
  5. Enter:
    • Name: Your Name
    • Email: demo@your-name.com
  6. Select Send through SMTP server:
    • SMTP: mail.your-name.com
    • Port: 465
    • Username: demo@your-name.com
    • Password: your password
    • Security: SSL
  7. Click Add Account and verify via email.

Note: This is a demo guide (ទ.៥ គឺជាគំរូ).
